//UME-Lite is a stripped fork of Brutal Doom's Map Enhancement System. Many
//upstream gore variants of vanilla Blood (MuchBlood, MediumBloodSpot, etc.)
//were removed alongside the gameplay systems that referenced them, but
//A_SpawnItemEx call sites for those classes still exist in this fork's gore
//chains (DEAD.txt, LIMBS.txt, MapSpecificDec.txt, mummy.txt, meat.txt).
//
//Rather than scatter deletions across every call site, the visually-oriented
//gore variants are aliased here to vanilla Blood. Each alias is a new class,
//not a "replaces" - gameplay-mod compatibility is preserved (per AGENTS.md
//section 4 "Replacement actors").
//
//Anything gameplay-affecting (BurnDamageSmall/Large, weapon carcasses,
//projectile trails, scoring pop-ups, head-explode gibs, terrain splashes)
//is intentionally NOT aliased here and is being deleted from its call sites
//in the corresponding per-file cleanup steps of the parent plan
//(decorate_unknown_class_triage).
